Who we are

The name Café Cà Phê was inspired by the Vietnamese word for coffee, "Cà phê" (pronounced Ka-fe). So the literal translation for it is Café Coffee. Here at Café Cà phê it is our mission to bring you authentic Vietnamese coffee, which is made with dark roast robusta beans. The robusta beans are known for their bold, earthy, bitter flavors and high caffeine content. Vietnam is now the largest producer of the robusta bean and the second-largest producer of coffee in the world.

Coffee was originally introduced into Vietnam in the year 1857 by the French. It is not served with milk, but with sweetened condensed milk instead due to the lack of dairy in Vietnamese culture.

We are open!

Here at Café Cà Phê We believe that coffee tastes better when you are with friends and family. So please come on in and join us for a cup today!
